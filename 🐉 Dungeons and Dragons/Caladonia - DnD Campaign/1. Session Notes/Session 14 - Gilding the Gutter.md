## 🎭 Major Design Features

### Cabaret Stage & Entertainment Hall
> Inspired by the **classic Moulin Rouge** aesthetic — velvet curtains, warm lighting, ornate decor.

- Main performance space for **event nights, galas, and musical acts**.  
- Tiered seating with tables for dining during shows.  
- Space doubles as a venue for special story events or faction gatherings.

---
### Bar, Tavern & Dining Areas
> A lively side lounge keeps the venue open even between performances.

- Separate **bar and eating area** adjacent to the main hall — open even when the stage isn’t in use.  
- Functions as an **“always-on” tavern**, keeping the venue active between major events.  
- **Layout suggestion:**
  - The **Main Hall** opens into the **Cabaret Stage** for large performances.  
  - A **side lounge** (separated by drapes or arches) houses the **bar and tapas tables**.  
  - Patrons can enjoy drinks casually or transition to the stage area when performances begin.  
- Ideal for downtime RP, side conversations, or espionage scenes.

---
### Party Living Quarters
> The venue is both a business and a home.

- **Private residential area** within the building.  
- Includes:
  - Personal rooms for each adventurer.  
  - A shared common space or lounge.  
  - Secret access to the smugglers’ tunnels below.  
- Makes the venue feel personal — *part home, part headquarters.*

---
### Guest & Performer Accommodation
> A small but useful feature for story hooks.

- **Short-term rooms** for travelers, performers, or temporary hires.  
- Styled as modest but comfortable tavern lodgings.  
- Great for introducing **mysterious guests, informants, or hidden enemies**.

---
### Smugglers’ Basement & Tunnel Network
> A hidden world beneath the club — half practical, half legend.

- Themed as a **“hidden underworld”** below the main floor.  
- Used for **smuggling goods in and out** unseen.  
- Contains an **unfinished experimental gas system** — originally a joke, now a potential hazard or tool.  
- Perfect for secret meetings, chases, or hiding illicit cargo.  
- Adds tension and mystery beneath the venue’s glamour.

---
## 💎 Style & Clientele

- **Target Audience:**  
  A confused mix of *everyone* — appealing to both **new money socialites** and **lower middle-class revelers**.  
  - Elegant façade with approachable undertones.  
  - The contrast creates an atmosphere of *refined chaos*.

- **Food:**  
  Curated by **Mirren**, a cook from *Dead Man’s Week*.  
  - Menu: **Exotic tapas-style small plates** designed for easy eating while drinking or dancing.

- **Drink:**  
  - **Basement brewhouse** for beer and ales.  
  - **In-house gin distillery** for signature cocktails.  
  - **Wine bar** in a quiet corner for the refined patrons.  
  - Focused on **craft, experimentation, and flavor**.

---
## 🧑‍🍳 Staff & Allies

### Trevor the Troll
- Recruited from *Dead Man’s Week*.  
- Acts as **bouncer and security**, gentle but intimidating.  
- His **wife** handles **cleaning, dishes, and general upkeep**.

---
### Mirren (Chef)
- Former cook from *Dead Man’s Week*.  
- Oversees the **kitchen, tapas menu, and cellar**.  
- Proud of her innovative small-plate cuisine.

---
### Snaggletooth & The Goblin Kids
- Streetwise goblins “cosplaying” as pirates.  
- Serve as **runners, helpers, and informants**.  
- Act as the party’s **eyes and ears in the streets** — not criminal, but morally flexible.  
- Great for flavor and intel gathering.

---
## 🕯️ Tone & Atmosphere

- **Ambience:** Low light, rich colors, warm candle glow, and faint gaslight haze.  
- **Sound:** Soft jazz or lute trios in quiet hours; wild cabaret by night.  
- **Scent:** Perfume, ale, roasted spices, and a faint sea tang from the harbor.  
- **Overall Feel:**  
  *A place of beauty and secrets — where laughter echoes above and whispers carry below.*
