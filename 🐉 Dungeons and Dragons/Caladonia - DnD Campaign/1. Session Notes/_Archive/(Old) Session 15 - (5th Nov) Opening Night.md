# 🕯️ Murder at the Grand Opening
*Session Outline for Port Reverie Campaign*

---
## [[📍 Setting | Port Reverie]]
The party’s newly opened **cabaret bar** sits in the heart of **Port Reverie**, a bustling neutral trade city where rival factions maintain an uneasy peace.  
The bar’s opening night draws nobles, merchants, and performers — the perfect backdrop for intrigue and disaster.

---
## ⚰️ The Crime
**Victim:** [[Alistair Vance]]  
A respected merchant and negotiator, found **poisoned** during the grand opening.  
He was finalizing a major trade alliance between the **Silver Consortium** and the **Azure League** — an alliance that would have shifted the balance of power.

---
## 🧩 Culprit & Motive
**Culprit:** [[Ironclad Company]]  
**Motive:** Maintain their monopoly on trade by sabotaging the Silver/Azure alliance and destabilizing the city.  
They arranged Alistair’s death during the opening to:
- Derail the alliance  
- Cast suspicion on the party  
- Reinforce their control of Port Reverie  

---
## 🔍 Investigation Flow

### 1️⃣ The Cabaret Scene
The party celebrates when **Alistair collapses** mid-toast.  
**Inspector Pym** seals the exits and begins questioning guests, including the party.

**Clues:**
- Tipped glass with trace poison  
- Note mentioning “unfinished business”  
- Witness accounts vary (Marilla, Lila, etc.)

---
### 2️⃣ The Hidden Tunnel
- [[Old Man Withers]] recalls an *old smuggling tunnel* under the bar.  
- Within, the party finds:
  - A **discarded glove** bearing the Ironclad insignia  
  - **Footprints** toward a concealed exit  
  - Residue from a **rare toxin** linked to Ironclad suppliers  

---
### 3️⃣ The Safe House
- Leads to a **neutral warehouse** used as a staging area.  
- Inside:
  - Letters between Ironclad agents  
  - A half-empty poison vial  
  - Evidence of payment to a fixer known as **“The Broker.”**

---
### 4️⃣ The Reveal
The party can:
- Expose Ironclad to the city council  
- Blackmail or ally with them  
- Keep the truth quiet for political advantage  

Each choice shifts **Port Reverie’s faction balance** going forward.

## 🧠 GM Notes

- Keep pacing brisk: **Social chaos → Murder → Investigation → Reveal**  
- Let players drive the investigation — each clue or NPC can lead anywhere.  
- Ironclad Company should remain powerful and menacing, not cartoonishly evil.  
- Outcomes shape future politics in Port Reverie: peace, chaos, or new alliances.
