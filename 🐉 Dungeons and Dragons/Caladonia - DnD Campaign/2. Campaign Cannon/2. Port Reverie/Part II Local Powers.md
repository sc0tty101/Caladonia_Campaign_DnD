# Part II: Local Powers

## The Marking House Consortium

**Motto:** _“All that is traded must be marked.”_
**Alignment:** Lawful Neutral
**Seat:** The Marking Hall, Crown Harbor

**Agenda:** Maintain monopoly on magical trade seals and authentication marks.
**Assets:** Bureaucratic enchanters, legal authority over all imports and exports.
**Methods:** Bureaucracy, red tape, magical surveillance.
**Relations:** Allied with the Syndicate of Coin; distrusted by pirates and smugglers.

**Player Hooks:**

- Smuggle unmarked goods past inspectors.

- Investigate forged trade marks undermining city economy.

- Recover a stolen master sigil granting trade immunity.


**Influence Actions:**

- Deliver contraband to them for registry (+1)

- Forge their marks (-1)


**DM Note:** The Marking House maintains order by drowning chaos in paperwork. Their bureaucrats wield ink like blades.

## The Concordat of Tides

**Motto:** _“Harmony in Exchange, Sanctity in Oath.”_
**Alignment:** Neutral Good
**Seat:** The Grand Chapel of Currents, Old Coins

**Agenda:** Uphold neutrality through sacred contracts and mediation.
**Assets:** Priests, oathbound scribes, sacred artifacts, the mystical _Sacred Ledger_.
**Methods:** Mediation, spiritual arbitration, magical compulsion of oaths.
**Relations:** Cooperative with the Neutrality Watch; adversarial to the Black Moon cult.

**Player Hooks:**

- Guard a sacred negotiation between rival factions.

- Retrieve a stolen page from the Sacred Ledger.

- Mediate between dueling merchants to prevent divine retribution.


**Influence Actions:**

- Fulfill a sworn contract (+1)

- Break a public oath (-1)


**DM Note:** The Concordat believes the city survives only because its oaths hold. They punish oathbreakers not with violence, but with divine silence.

---

## The Syndicate of Coin

**Motto:** _“Value defines truth.”_
**Alignment:** Lawful Neutral
**Seat:** The Vaulted Exchange, Old Coins

**Agenda:** Control currency, credit, and financial policy across nations.
**Assets:** Banks, investment houses, international creditors.
**Methods:** Debt manipulation, insider trading, selective bankruptcies.
**Relations:** Works with the Marking House, rivals the Workers’ Compact.

**Player Hooks:**

- Uncover a secret ledger revealing the Syndicate’s control of multiple governments.

- Protect a shipment of gold earmarked for emergency stabilization.

- Investigate mysterious disappearances of debtors.


**Influence Actions:**

- Repay or enforce major debts (+1)

- Support labor strikes (-1)


**DM Note:** The Syndicate is less a bank and more a religion of numbers—where belief in value makes it real.

---

## The Neutrality Watch

**Motto:** _“Balance Before Justice.”_
**Alignment:** Neutral
**Seat:** The Watchtower, Ninefold Hall

**Agenda:** Preserve Port Reverie’s neutrality at any cost.
**Assets:** Elite investigators, spies, magical surveillance network.
**Methods:** Observation, blackmail, precision enforcement.
**Relations:** Cooperative with the Concordat; suspicious of all others.

**Player Hooks:**

- Track a rogue investigator selling secrets to foreign powers.

- Assist in preventing a diplomatic assassination.

- Discover which Watch member is leaking council minutes.


**Influence Actions:**

- Report a breach of neutrality (+1)

- Exploit faction conflicts for profit (-1)


**DM Note:** The Watch doesn’t punish crimes—they prevent destabilization. A necessary evil in a city built on uneasy peace.

---

## The Cultural Preservation Society

**Motto:** _“Art Reveals All Truths.”_
**Alignment:** Neutral
**Seat:** The Grand Gallery, The Curtain

**Agenda:** Shape public opinion and gather intelligence through art and scholarship.
**Assets:** Artists, playwrights, critics, traveling performers, hidden agents.
**Methods:** Espionage via cultural exchange, coded messages in performances.
**Relations:** Friendly with the Order of the Silver Sun; covertly manipulates multiple factions.

**Player Hooks:**

- Uncover hidden intelligence in a painting before it’s sold abroad.

- Guard a playwright accused of treason.

- Decode a performance that doubles as a message between spies.


**Influence Actions:**

- Perform or sponsor cultural works (+1)

- Expose propaganda (-1)


**DM Note:** The Society speaks softly but writes loudly—its theater can start wars or end them.

---

## The Workers’ Compact

**Motto:** _“No one eats until all are fed.”_
**Alignment:** Chaotic Good
**Seat:** The Union Halls, Quiet Quays & The Sump

**Agenda:** Protect and empower laborers; resist exploitation by elites.
**Assets:** Thousands of workers, strike funds, underground networks.
**Methods:** Protests, mutual aid, coordinated strikes, and sabotage.
**Relations:** Allied with the Quiet Quays guilds; opposed by the Ironclads and the Syndicate.

**Player Hooks:**

- Prevent a protest from turning violent.

- Recover stolen union funds.

- Smuggle food into strike zones during crackdowns.


**Influence Actions:**

- Aid a labor action (+1)

- Undermine a strike (-1)


**DM Note:** The Compact wields solidarity like a weapon—when they move, the city stops.
