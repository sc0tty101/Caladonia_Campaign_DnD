**_Apothecary of Silkwater_**

Silkwater’s upper promenade, between jewelers and silk merchants. White stone frontage, frosted glass windows etched with botanical motifs. Guards pass often — not because of crime, but because of _clients_.

The door chime is soft silver, not a bell.

## Madame Lysenne Valeur

**Appearance**  
Immaculately dressed woman in her 40s. Pale gloves always worn. Hair pinned with alchemical glass needles. Smells faintly of rosewater and antiseptic.

**Manner**  
Measured, courteous, untouchable. She never raises her voice. She never rushes. She remembers _everything_.

**Reputation**
- Apothecary to nobles, courtesans, ambassadors
- Known for “discretion remedies”
- Officially spotless
- Socially feared

> “If Lysenne knows your ailment, she also knows your secrets.”
